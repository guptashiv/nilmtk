What is NILMTK?

To understand what Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring is? And why it's important? Go to the NILMTK Website. To keep it simple, NILMTK is a toolkit developed in python which first converts the data from various datasets collected in the passed to a uniform hd5 format. And then the advance analytics part of energy disaggregation can be applied consistently to any dataset irrespective of the original format it was recorded in. Out of all nilmtk dataset converters we will be dealing with nilmtk greend converter in detail.

What is GREEND dataset?

Going through NILMTK Website you might have come across all relevant dataset collected so far from some households etc. GREEND is one such dataset collected from 9 household with different appliances.
