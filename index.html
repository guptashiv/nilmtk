What is GREEND dataset?

Going through NILMTK Website you might have come across all relevant dataset collected so far from some households etc. GREEND is one such dataset collected from 9 household with different appliances.
The aim of Energy diassagregation is to know how much load is being contributed from each appliances towards the total Enrgy consumption.
